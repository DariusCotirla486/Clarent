# Clarent

Clarent is a privacy-oriented, AI-assisted system designed to support product managers during and after customer meetings. It bridges the gap between natural customer conversations and actionable engineering tasks, ensuring that valuable context is never lost in translation.

## The Problem

In a typical product workflow, translating customer feedback from a verbal discussion into precise, prioritized backlog items is a fragile process. This manual transition is time-consuming, prone to error, and vulnerable to context loss. Often, important details are missed, or requirements remain ambiguous, leading to rework and features that don't actually solve the customer's problem. 

## The Solution

Unlike standard meeting recorders that simply summarize a call after it ends, Clarent takes a proactive approach. It is designed to actively reduce ambiguity *while the conversation is still happening* and preserve that clarified context straight through to team execution.

### Core Features

* **Live Meeting Assistance:** Clarent joins consented online calls and acts as a silent assistant. It analyzes the conversation in real-time and generates targeted, clarifying questions for the product manager to ask if a customer's request appears ambiguous or underspecified.
* **Automated Task Generation:** After the meeting concludes, the system processes the complete transcript alongside your existing product context to generate structured, prioritized development tasks (complete with descriptions, rationale, and possible assignees).
* **Collaborative Dashboard:** A centralized workspace where product managers and development teams can review, edit, assign, and refine the generated tasks. It also handles workspace and meeting management.
